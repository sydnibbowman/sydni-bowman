Bob was a talented painter and brought smiles to many as he shared his talents witht the world

Robert Norman Ross was a painter art instructor and tv host. he was the creator and host of The joy of painting, a step by step tv program that aired from 1983 to 1994 on PBS in the united states. Ross went from being a public tv personality to being an internet celebrity in the 21st century with his talent and kindness leading to major popularity with fans everywhere many years after his death. Bobs art pieces have sold for about 10,000 dollars.
